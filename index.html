Questions and answers

What is a virus?

A virus is for example a program that gives another person accesses to do whatever they want on your computer. A virus has accesses to your computer when you click on it. A virus can copy itself and spread to other computer thru your wi-fi, like a normal virus.


What does a virus do?

A virus is on your computer all the time and can change everything without you knowing. A virus can destroy your important files and make your computer slower. A virus can be used for criminal things, like illegal pictures.


How do you stop a virus?

You can stop a virus by downloading an antivirus program, some costs money others are free. But the best you can do to stop a virus is by wiping your computer (deleting everything on the computer) you would normally only do this if your computer doesnt work.


Whitehathackers

Whitehathackers are hackers who hack for good. Whitehathackers are usually employed by large companies to try to hack the company, and then tells the information about where their security is weak. So, the company knows which of their security they need to work on.


Blackhathackers

Blackhathackers are the bad hackers. They are the ones who try to hack others and destroy their stuff. They are the opposite of whitehathackers. Blackhathackers try to steal files, account information from others and they talk you into giving them money to remove the virus, but they just leave it.
